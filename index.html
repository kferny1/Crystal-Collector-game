Crystal Game

Instructions

You will be given a random number to start the game

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game when you exceed the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

The value of each crystals will change each time the game starts.
